Shipping & Delivery Policy

At Study Capsule, all our products are digital and delivered electronically. There is no physical shipping involved in any of our orders.

01

Digital Delivery

  • All study materials including notes, mind maps, formula sheets and exam resources are delivered digitally.
  • Upon successful payment, you will be redirected to a page with your download link within a few minutes.
  • Download links are valid for 24 hours and allow 2 downloads. Please save and backup your files immediately after downloading.
  • No physical product will be shipped to your address.
02

Delivery Time

  • Digital products are delivered instantly or within a few minutes of payment confirmation
  • In rare cases of technical delays, delivery may take up to 24 hours
  • If you do not receive your product within 24 hours, please contact us immediately
03

No Physical Shipping

  • Study Capsule does not ship any physical products
  • No courier, postal or logistics services are used
  • No shipping charges are applied to any order
04

Delivery Issues

If you face any issue receiving your product:

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Ensure you entered the correct email address and phone number at checkout.
