Our Commitment

We offer a 7-day refund window from the date of purchase for eligible cases mentioned below. Since all our products are digital and delivered instantly, physical returns are not applicable.

02

Eligible Refund Cases

We gladly process refunds in the following situations:

  • Duplicate Payment — If you accidentally paid twice for the same product, we will refund the extra payment only after verification
  • Technical Delivery Issue — If you are unable to access or download your purchased material due to an issue on our end, we will address it or issue a refund if it remains unresolved.
  • Wrong Product Delivered — If you received a different product than what you ordered due to a system error, we will send the correct product or process a refund
03

Non-Eligible Refund Cases

Refunds will not be processed for:

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Incorrect selection of class, subject or product by the customer
  • Non-usage of the product after successful delivery
  • Technical issues arising due to the student’s device, internet connection, or software incompatibility.
04

Refund Process

  • To request a refund under the eligible conditions, you must contact our support team within 48 hours of purchase.
  • You may be asked to provide payment details, transaction ID, screenshots or screen recording videos to verify the issue.
  • Once approved and verified, the refund will be processed to your original payment method within 7–10 business days.
05

No Exchange Policy

  • Products once purchased cannot be exchanged.
  • For example, if you purchase a specific subject’s notes, they cannot later be exchanged for any other subject or product.
06

Customer Responsibilities

  • Ensure that you use a valid email address and phone number while purchasing, as digital products are delivered electronically.
  • Check your spam/junk folder if you do not see the download email in your inbox.
  • Save and back up your downloaded files after purchase since links may expire.
